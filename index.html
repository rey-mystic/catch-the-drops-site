Basics

What is an Airdrop?

An airdrop is a token distribution to early users who interact with a project. The core idea is simple: be early, be active, be real.

Beginner Track

Step-by-Step Guide (Preview)

1) Prepare your wallet — Use a burner wallet, keep main wallet safe.
2) Find legit projects — Official links only, no DM clicks.
3) Do on-chain tasks — Bridge, swap, LP, testnet quests.
4) Track progress — Notes/spreadsheets help a lot.
5) Claim safely — Check announcements, avoid phishing.
