TOKI Finance — Point Program & Airdrop Farming Guide

Only a few months left before the $TOKI token launches in Refine Phase (Season 1). Now is the best time to farm points through real on-chain activity.

Category: Mainnet Chain: Cosmos / IBC Difficulty: Medium Status: Live

What is TOKI Finance?

TOKI is a cross-chain bridge using IBC (Inter-Blockchain Communication). It lets you move assets across different chains in one click — fast, safe, and smooth. Their mission: build the Internet of Blockchains.

How TOKI Points Work

TOKI Points convert into $TOKI at the end of every Season.

Action Points

Earned from daily usage:

  • Transfers / Bridges
  • Liquidity Pools
  • Daily routines

Formula:
Action = (Transfer + Pool) × Bonus Boost

Loyalty Points

Earned from long-term contribution:

  • Testnet history
  • Discord Roles
  • Referrals

Formula:
Loyalty = Testnet + Role + Referral

Quick Setup (5 Minutes)

  1. Prepare a wallet (MetaMask, Keplr, OKX, etc.)
  2. Fund it for gas + transfers/LP
  3. Connect to Point Program: toki.finance/point-program?ref_code=GK6991
  4. Optional: enter referral code GK6991 for a boost

Fastest Method: Transfers / Bridge

Every transfer earns Transfer Points — simplest, fastest growth.

Open Transfer on TOKI App
Choose From/To chain (e.g., ETH → BNB)
Pick token and volume (bigger volume = bigger points)
Review fees → confirm
Points update automatically

Rough Formula:

Transfer Points ≈ USD Volume × Active Boost

Example: $1,000 transfer ≈ 1,000 points. With ×1.2 boost → 1,200 points.

Additional Method: Liquidity Pools

Deposit in TOKI single-sided pools for Pool Points. Points depend on early deposit, lock duration, and pool demand. Updates daily at 00:30 UTC.

Referrals & Free Points

  • Invitees get 5% boost (10% during campaigns).
  • Referrers earn 20% of Action Points weekly → become Loyalty Points every Wednesday.
  • Discord roles also add monthly Role Points.

No-Brainer Strategy

  • Do routine transfers with reasonable volume
  • Pick low gastime hours
  • Use & share referral code for boost
  • Add liquidity if you can lock
  • Check dashboard after TX
